About

Recently graduated with honours from the School of Interior Design at Ryerson University.

Graduated in 2017 with honours from the bachelor of fine arts program at the University of Ottawa, with specialization in photography and illustration.

As an aspiring commercial interior designer, I have always gravitated towards design which engages and incites interaction from its users. I enjoy questioning the canons of design and representation, while incorporating my fine art background into my designs. In order for me to have clarity of idea, I approach design as something which converts utility into behaviour, maximizing function and the human experience.
