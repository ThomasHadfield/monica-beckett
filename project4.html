Open/Closed: Rooms/Corridors

A series of corridor-ish rooms and room-ish corridors.
The 2019 Design Milk Stand, reimagines corridors and booths by combining walls, openings, and paths to produce a series of interconnected spaces. This project was in collaboration with Design Milk and Ryerson School of Interior Design, for the 2019 Toronto Interior Design Show.

Completed as part of a team with Ryerson University.
